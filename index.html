Why Choose KeiYomi?

Multi-Format Support

Seamlessly read PDF, CBZ, ZIP, and TXT files. Perfect for Manga, Manhwa, Comics, and Novels.

Flexible Reading Modes

Switch between Webtoon (Continuous Scroll) for manhwa and Normal (Paged) modes for traditional comics.

Local Library

Automatically scans your folder to organize your collection. Keeps track of your reading progress and history.

Customizable

Dark/Light themes, metadata editing, and favorites management. Tailor the app to your liking.

High Performance

Built with Electron and optimized with Lazy Loading for a smooth experience even on lower-end devices.

Open Source

100% Free and Open Source (MIT License). No ads, no tracking. Built by the community, for the community.

How It Works

KeiYomi is designed to be simple. Just point it to your document folder, and it does the rest.

1. Download

Get the latest release from GitHub and install it on your PC.

2. Add Files

Place your PDF/CBZ files in the Documents/KeiYomi folder.

3. Read

Open the app, let it scan, and enjoy your reading!
